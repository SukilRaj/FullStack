Game Overview

Marvel’s Spider-Man 2 is an action-adventure game developed by Insomniac Games exclusively for PlayStation 5. It continues the story of Peter Parker and Miles Morales as they protect New York City from powerful new threats, including Venom and Kraven the Hunter.

Game Features

Open World Exploration

The game expands the map to include Brooklyn and Queens, allowing players to swing through a larger and more detailed version of New York City.

Dual Spider-Men System

Players can instantly switch between Peter Parker and Miles Morales, each with unique abilities, skills, and combat styles.

Symbiote Powers

Peter gains powerful new abilities through the Venom symbiote, adding aggressive combat moves and special attacks to gameplay.
